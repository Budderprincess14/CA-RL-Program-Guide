General Information

What is Rocket League?

In the simplest of terms... it is car soccer. Teams of three will compete in 5 minute games to see who can score the most goals. This is done by using their car to bump and shoot a giant ball into the goal. The first team to score 3 wins will be the winner of that day's matchup.

How You Can Support the Teams

  • Compliment their cars/goal explosions when they score
  • What a save!/Good block or defense!
  • Nice rotation
  • Impressive/amazing aerial or air dribble (these are difficult skills)
  • Nice demo! (when a player collides with another car and it explodes)
  • Great teamwork! (when passing the ball/positioning to score)

Common Rocket League Terms

You can find some of the most common terms used in Rocket League here.

Common Gaming Terms

  • gg - good game (after the game to show good sportsmanship)
  • glhf - good luck have fun (before the game)
  • pog - Play Of the Game; said when someone does something cool or does a "pro gamer" move
